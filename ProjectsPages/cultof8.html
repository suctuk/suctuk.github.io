CultOf8 Website

Created a shop site to sell stickers for a Creative Collective in Boulder called, Cultof8.

Created a shop site to sell stickers for a Creative Collective in Boulder called, Cultof8.
Was asked to take inspiration from odd-culture references like mschf.com, Heaven's Gate, and Odd Future.
Creative Director: Rowan Dwyer, requested that the website "Look like it could hack you"
Used 3rd party app: Ecwid to host shop backend and to help maintain product and sale data.
Used 3d creations and doodles from local Creatives Aidan Roof, Rowan, and Luke Chabin.
Prototyped and planned user interaction and experience using Figma, and VSCode with Rowan, Luke, and Kai Franks.
Site hosted through GitHub pages.
Worked with Rowan on back-end development through Ecwid.

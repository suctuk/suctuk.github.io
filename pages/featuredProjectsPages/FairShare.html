Fair Share, App Wireframe

This was a group project for my Human Computer Interaction class. We were tasked with utilizing tools like Figma to build a wireframe for an app idea that solves an issue for a specific population. Our issue was keeping track of splitting bills at restaurants for people in friendly or romantic relationships. The target user would likely be Gen Z or Millennials who use apps like Venmo, and Zelle to pay people. This app lets people calculate shares for each party member and send money requests in the same interface.

Process & Details

Here is the Low-Fidelity Prototype. I was in charge of animating different functions like the user input fields, and scroll functions in Figma prototypes as well as setting up the main Home Page interface and button flows.

DIY Sk*dis from Ik*a

This was a personal project for my apartment kitchen. I wanted to have a means to organize my kitchen since I do not have a lot of counter space. I can 3d print attachments to hold different objects.

Process & Details

Model in Illustrator

This took me the longest to do. I messed up the file 4 or 5 times. I was really nervous that the dimensions would be off. I then imported this file into Rhino to cut

Laser Cutting

The initial cut took an hour. The wood I used was warped so I had to edit my illustrator file to recut the holes that didn't go all the way through.

Staining

I used a oil based and polyurethane mixed stain. I did 2 coats on the front and one on the back. It smelled terrible and stunk up my place for 2 days. But I like the color.
